Mastering Public Speaking

Ages 5–10 2–3 hours Instructor: Optional

Course Description

Public speaking doesn't have to be scary! In this fun and interactive course, kids will explore the basics of communication, overcome their fear of speaking, and build confidence to stand and speak in front of an audience. Through simple techniques and creative storytelling, young learners will practice organizing their ideas and delivering short speeches that are exciting, clear, and impactful.

Basics of Communication

Managing Stage Fright

Speech Crafting

Engaging Audiences

Connect with your listeners and respond smoothly

Visual Aids & Storytelling

Support your message with images and great stories
