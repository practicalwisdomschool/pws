Understanding Net Banking & UPI

Ages 5-10 1-2 hours Instructor: Optional

Course Description

Discover the exciting world of digital payments! This engaging course introduces young learners to Net Banking and UPI (Unified Payments Interface). Students will explore how online banking works, learn about UPI apps, understand basic security measures, and gain confidence in navigating the digital financial world. Through interactive demonstrations, simple activities, and guided discussions, children will develop essential financial literacy skills for the digital age!

#DigitalPayments #NetBanking #UPI #FinancialLiteracy

What You'll Learn

Net Banking Basics

Understand online banking services and applications

UPI Transactions

Learn how UPI works and its transaction methods

Online Security

Recognize common scams and learn protection measures

Card Transactions

Use debit and credit cards safely for online payments
