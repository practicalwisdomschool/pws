Use of Credit Card

Ages 5-10 1-2 hours Instructor: Optional

Course Description

This introductory financial literacy course helps young learners understand what credit cards are and how they work. Through engaging activities and simple explanations, children will learn the basics of credit cards, including different types, benefits, potential disadvantages, and the concept of credit scores. The course provides a foundation for responsible financial habits at an early age.

#FinancialLiteracy #CreditCards #MoneyManagement #ResponsibleSpending

What You'll Learn

Credit Card Basics

Learn what a credit card is, how it works, and who can use one

Types of Credit Cards

Become familiar with different types of credit cards

Benefits & Disadvantages

Understand the advantages and potential downsides of credit cards

Credit Scores

Introduction to what credit scores are and why they matter
