Course Description

Master the art of document creation with our Basics of Word course! This comprehensive introduction helps young learners discover the fundamentals of Microsoft Word. Students will learn how to navigate the Word interface, format text and content effectively, work with images and tables, and use reviewing tools. Through practical exercises and guided activities, they will build essential skills for academic projects, reports, and future professional writing tasks!
