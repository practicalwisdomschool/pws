About Sarah

I'm a professional photographer with over 10 years of experience capturing moments that tell powerful stories. My passion for photography began when I was just 12 years old, which is why I'm especially passionate about teaching young photographers how to develop their own unique visual voice.

I specialize in portrait and landscape photography, with a particular focus on natural lighting techniques. My work has been featured in National Geographic, Vogue, and various photography exhibitions across North America.

When teaching children, I focus on making photography fun and accessible while building a strong foundation of technical skills. I believe every child has a unique perspective to share with the world, and I'm here to help them express that vision through photography.

Experience & Achievements

Award-Winning Photographer

Winner of the 2023 International Photography Awards in the Portrait Category and recipient of the Young Photographer Mentor Award for my work with children.

Published Photographer

My work has been featured in National Geographic, Vogue, and various photography exhibitions across North America. I've published two books on photography techniques for beginners.

Educational Background

MFA in Photography from Rhode Island School of Design and a certification in Child Education from the International Teaching Association.

Teaching Approach

I believe in a hands-on, project-based approach that makes learning photography fun and engaging. My teaching style adapts to each child's interests and learning pace, ensuring they build confidence while developing technical skills.
